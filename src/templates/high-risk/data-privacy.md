# Data Privacy Guidelines

## Principles
- Collect only necessary data.
- Store data securely with encryption at rest and in transit.
- Provide transparency and control to users.

## Required Practices
- Maintain a privacy policy.
- Implement data access requests.
- Support data deletion workflows.

## Security Controls
- Role-based access control (RBAC).
- Audit logging for sensitive data access.
- Regular privacy impact reviews.
