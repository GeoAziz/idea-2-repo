# Incident Response Plan

## Objectives
- Detect and respond to security incidents quickly.
- Minimize impact to users and systems.
- Preserve evidence for forensic analysis.

## Response Phases
1. **Preparation**: Define roles, tooling, and runbooks.
2. **Detection**: Monitor alerts, logs, and anomaly reports.
3. **Containment**: Isolate affected systems and revoke compromised access.
4. **Eradication**: Remove threats and patch vulnerabilities.
5. **Recovery**: Restore systems and validate integrity.
6. **Post-Incident Review**: Document findings and improve controls.

## Communication Plan
- Internal: Engineering, Security, and Leadership.
- External: Customers, regulators, and stakeholders (as required).

## Checklist
- [ ] Identify incident severity
- [ ] Assign incident commander
- [ ] Document timeline and evidence
- [ ] Notify impacted parties
- [ ] Execute remediation plan
